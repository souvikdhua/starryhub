=== SECTION 4: PLANETARY ASPECTS (GRAHA DRISHTI) ===
[Source: BPHS Ch.26]
All planets aspect the 7th house from their position (full aspect 100%).
Mars has special aspects: 4th house (full), 7th house (full), 8th house (full) from its position.
Jupiter has special aspects: 5th house (full), 7th house (full), 9th house (full) from its position.
Saturn has special aspects: 3rd house (full), 7th house (full), 10th house (full) from its position.
Rahu and Ketu: Same aspects as their dispositor planet (disputed). Some authorities give them 5th, 7th, 9th aspects like Jupiter.

=== SECTION 5: COMBUSTION (ASTANGATA) ===
[Source: BPHS, Surya Siddhanta]
A planet is combust when within these orbs of the Sun:
Moon: 12°. Mars: 17°. Mercury: 14° (12° when retrograde). Jupiter: 11°. Venus: 10° (8° when retrograde). Saturn: 15°.
Effects: Combust planet loses power to deliver results of houses it owns. The native struggles to express that planet's significations externally, though internal awareness remains. Combust planet in own sign or exalted: combustion is mitigated. Combust planet in enemy sign: worst results, complete loss of signification.
Critical rule: A combust planet conjunct Sun within 1° is called "Asta" (completely burnt) and is extremely weakened.

=== SECTION 6: RETROGRESSION (VAKRI) ===
[Source: Phaladeepika, Saravali]
Retrograde planets are powerful. A retrograde planet gives results of the previous house. Retrograde benefic (Jupiter/Venus/Mercury) acts like an exalted planet if placed well. Retrograde malefic (Mars/Saturn) intensifies malefic significations but gives unexpected positive results in Upachaya houses (3,6,10,11). Retrograde planet in exaltation acts as debilitated. Retrograde planet in debilitation acts as exalted (Vakri Neecha Bhanga). Retrograde planet in its own sign: highly empowered, gives delayed but supreme results.

=== SECTION 7: HOUSE SIGNIFICATIONS (BHAVA KARAKATVA) ===
[Source: BPHS Ch.11-12, Phaladeepika Ch.1-13]

HOUSE 1 (Lagna/Tanu Bhava): Physical body, health, vitality, personality, appearance, head, brain, self-identity, fame, overall life direction. Natural karaka: Sun. Strong 1st lord = strong constitution and willpower.

HOUSE 2 (Dhana Bhava): Accumulated wealth, family lineage (kutumba), speech quality, food habits, right eye, face, early education, values, death (maraka sthana). Natural karaka: Jupiter. Malefic here damages family harmony and speech.

HOUSE 3 (Sahaja Bhava): Younger siblings, courage (parakrama), short travels, communication skills, writing, hands/arms/shoulders, hobbies, self-effort, neighbors. Natural karaka: Mars. Malefics perform well here (Upachaya).

HOUSE 4 (Sukha Bhava): Mother, happiness, homeland, vehicles, real estate, education (formal), chest/heart/lungs, emotional inner peace, throne/authority seat. Natural karaka: Moon,Mercury. Afflicted 4th = restless mind.

HOUSE 5 (Putra Bhava): Children, intelligence (buddhi), creativity, romance, past-life merit (purva punya), mantras, speculation/stocks, stomach, higher learning, disciples. Natural karaka: Jupiter. Trikona house = always auspicious.

HOUSE 6 (Ripu/Roga Bhava): Enemies, diseases, debts, litigation, maternal uncle, service/employment, competitive exams, intestines, obstacles. Natural karaka: Mars,Saturn. Malefics here destroy enemies. Dusthana + Upachaya.

HOUSE 7 (Kalatra Bhava): Spouse, marriage, business partnerships, foreign travel, public dealings, lower abdomen, sexual organs, trade, maraka sthana (death-inflicting). Natural karaka: Venus. 7th lord dignity = marriage quality.

HOUSE 8 (Ayu Bhava): Longevity, death circumstances, occult sciences, hidden knowledge, chronic diseases, inheritance, in-laws' wealth, sudden events, transformation, sexual vitality, reproductive organs. Natural karaka: Saturn. Most malefic dusthana.

HOUSE 9 (Bhagya/Dharma Bhava): Father, fortune (bhagya), guru/teacher, long pilgrimages, higher philosophy, religion, law, hips/thighs, past-life dharma, divine grace. Natural karaka: Jupiter,Sun. Greatest trikona = supreme blessing house.

HOUSE 10 (Karma Bhava): Career, profession, fame, public reputation, government authority, knees, actions in society, father's status, karma in this life. Natural karaka: Sun,Mercury,Jupiter,Saturn. Strongest kendra.

HOUSE 11 (Labha Bhava): Gains, income, elder siblings, fulfillment of desires, social networks, ankles/calves, awards, recognition, large organizations. Natural karaka: Jupiter. Greatest Upachaya = anything here gains over time.

HOUSE 12 (Vyaya Bhava): Losses, expenses, foreign lands/settlement abroad, moksha/spiritual liberation, bed pleasures, left eye, feet, hospitals, prisons, isolation, meditation, charity, sleep quality. Natural karaka: Saturn,Ketu. Dusthana but also moksha sthana.

=== SECTION 8: NEECHA BHANGA RAJA YOGA ===
[Source: BPHS Ch.36, Phaladeepika Ch.7]
Cancellation of debilitation converts weakness into extraordinary strength. Conditions (any one suffices):
1. Lord of the sign where planet is debilitated is in kendra from Lagna or Moon.
2. Lord of the exaltation sign of the debilitated planet is in kendra from Lagna or Moon.
3. The debilitated planet is aspected by the lord of its debilitation sign.
4. The debilitated planet is conjunct the planet that is exalted in that sign.
5. The debilitated planet is in mutual aspect with its exalted sign lord.
6. The debilitated planet is in kendra from Lagna.
7. The debilitated planet exchanges signs with another planet (Parivartana Yoga).
Result: Native faces extreme hardship early in life but achieves spectacular rise later, especially during the dasha of the Neecha Bhanga planet.

=== SECTION 9: MAJOR RAJA YOGAS ===
[Source: BPHS Ch.36-41, Phaladeepika]

DHARMA-KARMADHIPATI YOGA: Lords of 9th and 10th conjunct or in mutual aspect. Highest career yoga. Native achieves supreme professional status and righteous wealth.

GAJAKESARI YOGA: Jupiter in kendra (1,4,7,10) from Moon. Gives lasting fame, wisdom, learning, wealth, and many children. Strength depends on Jupiter's dignity and aspects.

PANCHA MAHAPURUSHA YOGAS (5 Great Persons):
- RUCHAKA YOGA: Mars in own sign or exalted in kendra. Warrior, commander, fearless, mark on body.
- BHADRA YOGA: Mercury in own sign or exalted in kendra. Scholar, eloquent, long life, sharp intellect.
- HAMSA YOGA: Jupiter in own sign or exalted in kendra. Righteous king, beautiful body, devotee of gods.
- MALAVYA YOGA: Venus in own sign or exalted in kendra. Beautiful, wealthy, learned, sensual pleasures, fine vehicles.
- SASHA YOGA: Saturn in own sign or exalted in kendra. Commands servants, leader of village/town, wicked disposition but powerful.

VIPARITA RAJA YOGA: Lord of 6th, 8th, or 12th placed exclusively in another dusthana (6,8,12) without benefic conjunction or aspect. Sudden rise through destruction of enemies or after catastrophic failure. Most powerful when 8th lord is in 12th or 6th lord is in 8th.

DHANA YOGA (Wealth): Lords of 2nd and 11th connected by conjunction, mutual aspect, or exchange. Enhanced if 5th/9th lords also involved. Multiple dhana yogas = multi-millionaire potential.

BUDHADITYA YOGA: Sun and Mercury conjunct. Intelligent, skilled, good reputation. ONLY effective if Mercury is not combust (>14° from Sun) and both are in good dignity.

CHANDRA-MANGALA YOGA: Moon and Mars conjunct or in mutual aspect. Wealth through courage and bold enterprise. Woman's chart: passionate nature, strong will.

ADHI YOGA: Benefics (Jupiter, Venus, Mercury) in 6th, 7th, and 8th from Moon. Gives political power, polite speech, trustworthy nature, destroyer of enemies, long life.

LAKSHMI YOGA: Lord of 9th is strong and in kendra/trikona, and Venus is in own/exalted sign in kendra/trikona. Immense wealth, beautiful spouse, virtuous character.

SARASWATI YOGA: Jupiter, Venus, Mercury in kendras, trikonas, or 2nd house, with Jupiter in own/exalted/friendly sign. Mastery of arts, music, poetry, scriptures. Highly learned.

=== SECTION 10: MALEFIC YOGAS (DOSHAS) ===
[Source: BPHS, Phaladeepika]

KALA SARPA YOGA: All 7 planets (Sun through Saturn) hemmed between Rahu and Ketu axis. Gives struggle, sudden rises and falls, karmic intensity. If Rahu is leading (all planets after Rahu before Ketu going zodiacally): Kala Sarpa. If Ketu leads: Kala Amrita Yoga (less malefic).

MANGALA DOSHA (Kuja Dosha): Mars in 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house from Lagna, Moon, or Venus. Creates obstacles in marriage, aggression in partnerships. Cancelled if: Mars is in own sign/exalted, or spouse also has Mangala Dosha, or Jupiter aspects Mars, or Mars is in Aries/Scorpio/Capricorn in those houses.

KEMDRUM YOGA: Moon with no planet in 2nd or 12th from it, and no planet in kendra from Moon or Lagna. Extreme poverty, struggle, loneliness, mental anguish. Cancelled if Moon is aspected by Jupiter or Venus, or if Moon is in kendra.

GRAHAN YOGA: Sun/Moon conjunct Rahu or Ketu. Eclipse energy in the chart. Sun-Rahu: father problems, ego crises, government troubles. Sun-Ketu: spiritual detachment from father, identity confusion. Moon-Rahu: anxiety, obsessive mind, mother issues. Moon-Ketu: psychic abilities, emotional detachment, spiritual inclination.

SHAKATA YOGA: Jupiter in 6th, 8th, or 12th from Moon. Fluctuating fortune, periods of great success alternating with poverty. Cancelled if Jupiter is in kendra from Lagna.

DARIDRA YOGA (Poverty): Lord of 11th in 6th, 8th, or 12th. Gains are undermined, income through debts or illegal means. Worsened if 2nd lord is also afflicted.

=== SECTION 12: VIMSHOTTARI DASHA SYSTEM ===
[Source: BPHS Ch.46-49, Phaladeepika Ch.20]

Total cycle: 120 years. Order and periods:
Ketu=7 years, Venus=20 years, Sun=6 years, Moon=10 years, Mars=7 years, Rahu=18 years, Jupiter=16 years, Saturn=19 years, Mercury=17 years.

Starting dasha determined by Moon's nakshatra at birth:
Ketu nakshatras: Ashwini, Magha, Moola.
Venus nakshatras: Bharani, Purva Phalguni, Purva Ashadha.
Sun nakshatras: Krittika, Uttara Phalguni, Uttara Ashadha.
Moon nakshatras: Rohini, Hasta, Shravana.
Mars nakshatras: Mrigashira, Chitra, Dhanishtha.
Rahu nakshatras: Ardra, Swati, Shatabhisha.
Jupiter nakshatras: Punarvasu, Vishakha, Purva Bhadrapada.
Saturn nakshatras: Pushya, Anuradha, Uttara Bhadrapada.
Mercury nakshatras: Ashlesha, Jyeshtha, Revati.

DASHA INTERPRETATION RULES:
1. Mahadasha lord sets the THEME of the period based on houses it owns.
2. Antardasha lord delivers SPECIFIC EVENTS based on its house ownership, placement, and relationship with Mahadasha lord.
3. If Antardasha lord is 6th or 8th from Mahadasha lord: disease, enmity, accidents, friction (Shadashtaka).
4. If Antardasha lord is 2nd or 12th from Mahadasha lord: loss, expenditure, change of place.
5. If Antardasha lord is 5th or 9th from Mahadasha lord: high fortune, children, spiritual merit (Trikona).
6. If Antardasha lord is in mutual kendras (1,4,7,10) with Mahadasha lord: productive action, support.
7. Dasha of yogakaraka planet: best period of life. Dasha of functional malefic: challenging period.
8. Dasha of maraka planet (lord of 2nd or 7th): health crises, near-death experiences, actual death in old age.

=== SECTION 13: JAIMINI CHARA KARAKAS ===
[Source: Jaimini Sutras Ch.1]
Determined by descending degree in sign (excluding Rahu):
1. Atma Karaka (AK): Highest degree. Soul's desire. King of chart. Its sign in D9 = Karakamsa.
2. Amatya Karaka (AmK): 2nd highest. Career, profession, sustenance. Minister.
3. Bhratru Karaka (BK): 3rd highest. Siblings, guru, courage.
4. Matru Karaka (MK): 4th highest. Mother, property, vehicles, heart.
5. Putra Karaka (PK): 5th highest. Children, intelligence, creativity.
6. Gnati Karaka (GK): 6th highest. Enemies, disease, obstacles. The adversary.
7. Dara Karaka (DK): Lowest degree. Spouse, partnerships.

KARAKAMSA ANALYSIS:
10th from Karakamsa: supreme career direction.
Benefic in 1st from Karakamsa: spiritual, learned.
Sun in Karakamsa: government work. Moon: trade in liquids. Mars: fire, weapons, police. Mercury: business, commerce. Jupiter: philosophy, teaching, dharma. Venus: politics, poetry, arts. Saturn: traditional occupation.
12th from Karakamsa: path to moksha. Ketu here = moksha assured. Saturn here = devoted to God.

AQUARIUS LAGNA: Yogakaraka=Venus (4th+9th lord). Kendra+Trikona. Mars (3rd+10th) and Jupiter (2nd+11th) give mixed results.
PISCES LAGNA: No single yogakaraka. Mars (2nd+9th) and Moon (5th) are most benefic. Mercury (4th+7th) is maraka.

=== SECTION 21: PLANETARY DIGNITY SCORING (SHADBALA PRINCIPLES) ===

=== SECTION 22: ASHTAKAVARGA SYSTEM ===
[Source: BPHS Ch.66-72]
Each planet contributes benefic points (bindus) to signs. Maximum 8 bindus per sign per planet.
Total points per sign across all planets: Sarvashtakavarga (SAV). Maximum 56 per sign.
SAV > 30 in a sign: Extremely strong house. Planets transiting here give excellent results.
SAV 25-30: Good results. SAV < 25: Weak house, planets transiting give poor results.
SAV < 20: Very weak house — any transit here brings suffering.
Individual planet's Bhinnashtakavarga: > 4 bindus = good transit results. < 4 = bad transit results.
KEY RULE: Saturn transiting a sign with SAV < 25 during Sade Sati = worst period.
Jupiter transiting a sign with SAV > 28 = phenomenal fortune.

=== SECTION 39: DISPOSITOR CHAIN ===
[Source: Advanced Parashari Analysis]
Every planet is "disposed" by the lord of the sign it occupies.
Following the dispositor chain reveals the FINAL DISPOSITOR — the planet that ultimately controls the chart.
If a planet is in its own sign: It is self-disposed and becomes a final dispositor.
If two planets are in each other's signs: Mutual reception (Parivartana Yoga) — they share power.
A chart with a single final dispositor: Life has clear direction. That planet's significations dominate.
A chart with a mutual reception as final dispositor: Life oscillates between two themes.
